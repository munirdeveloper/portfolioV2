Harry Potter: Sorting Hat

The Harry Potter series is a childhood favorite of mine. What fascinated me in the first movie was the mysterious Sorting Hat.

To brush up on a new technology and my programming skills, I built a fullstack website where Harry Potter fans can take a Sorting Hat quiz, and find out which House they belong to!

Built with Svelte.js and Tailwind.
